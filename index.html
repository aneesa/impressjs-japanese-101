Cultures
Name Suffixes
> San --> Polite (New acquaintance, Friends)
> Sama --> Respect (Brother, Customers)
> Kun --> (Close males friends, Female subordinate)
> Chan --> (Close friends (m/f), Younger male)
Train
> Buy tickets at the machines
> Tickets are sold by the prices, not by the destinations
> Every train station has a price map
> Machines often look more complicated than they are
> Drop your coins in the slots, and press the correct price button
Local Bus
> Enter through the middle door, take the numbered ticket
> Check your bus fare from a screen display at the front of the bus
> Your fare corresponds to the number on the ticket
> Pay the exact amount - coin change machine is provided for 1000 yen bill
> Get off through the front door
